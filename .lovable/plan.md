1. THE SERIES BIBLE (1982) - Michael Halperin wrote it December 1, 1982. This is the foundational document. It established Adam's origin before any episode aired.

2. ADAM'S ORIGIN FROM THE SERIES BIBLE:
- Adam was walking through the "Wind Forest" on Eternia
- A FALCON was flying overhead, screeching at him, LEADING him somewhere
- He followed the bird through an "adventurous path" to a clearing
- He looked up and saw Castle Grayskull for the first time
- The falcon TRANSFORMED into the Sorceress

- The Jaw Bridge had NOT been opened for CENTURIES - she opened it for him
- Inside the castle he was given the Power Sword
- She told him to STRIKE THE SWORD AGAINST STONE and say "By the power of Grayskull!"
- He transformed into He-Man

3. ADAM'S BLOODLINE - Half Eternian, Half TERRAN (Earth human). His mother Queen Marlena was an Earth astronaut who CRASH-LANDED on Eternia. Adam grew up to be "smarter, more agile, stronger, and more muscular than any Eternian" — meaning the HALF-EARTH blood was the upgrade.

4. THE SECRET - Only Man-At-Arms, Orko, and Zodac knew. The reason the secret must be kept: "if Skeletor or other evil forces knew, then that knowledge would endanger all of those whom he loves."
